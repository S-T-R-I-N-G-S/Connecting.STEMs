About us

We are a group of students passionate about gender equality, and the representation of women within the field of STEM

We are also intrigued by Computer Science and coding ourselves.

When presented with the opportunity we decided to bring both our interests together and create a blog.

This aims to educate young minds and inculcate values of equality within other students from a young age, as well as to raise awareness about the critical issue of a lack of representation and stereotypes that exist about women in STEM.
